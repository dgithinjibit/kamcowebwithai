Welcome from the Chief Principal

At Kamwenja Teachers' College, we've been a centre of excellence in teacher education since 1924. Our mission is simple: to provide quality training that blends academic rigor, creativity, innovation, and strong moral values.

We are proud of our tradition, our dedicated staff, and our vibrant student community. Here, learning extends beyond the classroom, with opportunities in sports, clubs, and service to help every student grow as a well-rounded professional.

We invite you to explore our programs, connect with our community, and see how we're shaping the future of education.

Mr. Wycliffe Wafula
Chief Principal / Secretary B.O.M
